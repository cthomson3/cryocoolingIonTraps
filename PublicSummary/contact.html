About Me

My name is Christopher Thomson and I am a final year MPhys Student at the University of Edinburgh. My academic interests lie in nuclear physics and hands on experimental work. This website forms part of my MPhys project, which focuses on the cryocooling of a novel printed circuit board radio-frequency quadrupole (PCB RFQ) ion trap. For my public summary I wished to provide an accessible overview of my work to those with limited physics knowledge.
